About Me

I'm a student the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, with a major in C.S. and a minor in Mathematics. I'm set to graduate in December 2018. I'm primarily interested in backend software development, and have extensive experience working in C, Java, and Python. I'm also proficient with Javascript, C#, and Ruby.

I was born and grew up in St. Petersburg, Russia, and became an American citizen at the age of 15. Since then, I've lived near Chicago, IL. In my free time, I enjoy reading, discussing politics, and being out in nature. When I have the opportunity, I love to travel. Last spring, I spent six months abroad in Stockholm, Sweden, studying at KTH Royal Institute of Technology.
